My Story

Fun fact: I've never lived longer than 5 years in the same city.

  • 1997

    Enter Stage

    I was born in Quezon City, Philippines - the land of jeepneys and mangoes. According to home family videos, I grew up speaking English + Tagalog = Taglish (have evolved to English now but can still understand the mother tongue of my parents well enough to eavesdrop on private conversations.)

  • 2000-2009

    Canadian, Eh?

    My family immigrated to Vancouver when I was 3 years old; we lived in beautiful BC for 5 years where I learned to enjoy playing outside (have kept this trait as an adult) and then 4 years in Calgary where I developed a private taste for country music (again kept mostly private so please don't worry).

  • 2009-2014

    Back Home (?)

    As I entered the tumultous teenage years, my family returned to the Philippines temporarily. Our 5 years there were some of the most challenging and rich years of my life as I learned more about myself and about the world, experiencing life in a developing nation as a young adult.

  • 2015-2019

    La Belle Province

    I spent the last year of high school back in Vancouver (this was also the year I first learned and fell in love with programming) and then moved to Montreal, QC to study computer science at McGill University. My time here has been made rich by amazing people, culture, opportunities, and food. I've enjoyed picking up French and have also learned a thing or two at school.

  • 2018-Present

    MPCL

    In the summer of 2018, I was hired as web application developer to the Music Perception and Cognition Lab at McGill University. It was largely through my time working for MPCL that I gained an interest in web and software development. It has been so cool to learn and experiment with REST, Django, and the full stack (from database models and front-end HTML).

Orchard Database Web App

Database web application for the Orchestration and Perception Project

Orchard - the Orchestration Analysis and Research Database - is a web application built on Django, Solr, PostgreSQL stack for the Music Perception and Cognition Lab at McGill University. I currently work to develop features for and maintain the application.

Hogwarts Campus Network

Configuration of Hogwarts campus network with Packet Tracer

In this project, we designed and configured a university's campus network including IPv4 and IPv6 addressing, static and dynamic routing, wireless access, and configuring and testing applications.

  • Co-Contributors: Haoyi Zhao, Su Yu

Bank Database Application

PostgreSQL database application for banking institutions

In this project, we developed and built a database application for banking institutions. We performed requirement analysis, designed an ER schema, created our database using PostgreSQL, and wrote a simple user-friendly application program for the database in Java.

  • Co-contributors: Minh Anh Nguyen, Sisi Lao, Tianyi Wu

AI Pentago Player

Artificial intelligence Pentago player with minimax, alpha-beta pruning, and iterative deepening

In this final project for COMP 424, I built an artificial intelligence player for the two-player game Pentago-Swap. An implementation of the game was given and the majority of work was done simply in the Student class where the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning and iterative deepening was implemented.
